    Gameplay rules:


  • Do not RDM (Random Death Match) or take revenge for RDM
  • Killing a fellow teammate on purpose is considered RDM, and is punished with TWO SLAYS
  • As innocent, throwing inciendary grenades or discobombs is considered RDM
  • As innocent, using props to kill, or attempt to kill is forbidden
  • Prop-surfing is allowed, and anyone is allowed to shoot prop-surfers, but only when mid-air
  • ONLY players chosen as detective can issue orders to other players, and can injure players who disobey
  • No T-Baiting, this involves doing any of the traitorous acts as an innocent and/or trying to make other players RDM
  • Camping a specific area of the map is tolerated only for 30 seconds maximum, and claiming a room or an area is forbidden

    • Server rules:


  • Ghosting is stricly forbidden, users will be banned permanently
  • Killing AFK players is forbidden, mention them to staff in the chat
  • Spamming the text or voice chat is forbidden (this includes advertising)
  • All players must respect each other, any form of discremination (race, gender, age...) and harassment is forbidden
  • Pornographic or graphical sprays will result in a kick / ban
  • Your username must be taggable and pronounceable
  • Your karma (which is seen by presing TAB) must stay as high as possible, low karma players will be banned

    • Interacting with staff


  • You may report any player on the server with '!report', false reports will be punished
  • Reports and replies to reports must be written in english, and any reply without a reason in english will result in a slay
  • The staff follows stricts instructions to respond to each offence
